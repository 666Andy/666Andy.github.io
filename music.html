What are Apple Loops?

Apple Loops are prerecorded musical phrases or riffs in the Loop Browser that you can use to easily add drum beats, rhythm parts, and other sounds to a project. These loops contain musical patterns that can be repeated over and over, and can be extended to fill any amount of time. When you add an Apple Loop to a project, a region is created for the loop. When the project plays, the region plays at the projectâ€™s tempo and key. This behavior lets you use several loops together, even if the loops were recorded at different speeds and in different keys.
